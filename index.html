Who we are

Hope Paws is a non-profit focused on rescue, medical care, and adoption. Your support funds food, shelter, and vet bills.Founded in 2020, our rescue team works tirelessly with volunteers, veterinarians, and loving families to rehome animals. With your support, we can continue to make a difference in their lives.
